Ridley Scott is an English film director and producer who has been key to many legendary movies particularly in the Sci-Fi genre. Scott was born on November 30, 1937 in Durham, England, UK. He has two brothers, and his brother Tony Scott also went in the film business.

In Scott?s early years he went to West Hartlepool College of Art graduating in 1958 with a diploma of design. He went on the London?s Royal College of Art where he produced a short film, Boy and Bicycle. From there he worked as a trainee with the BBC as a set designer in 1963. He was involved in the direction of a couple of series at the BBC.

Scott established his own film and commercial production company in 1968, Ridley Scott Associates (RSA). Under this banner, he has had an enormous influence on popular film culture, producing many films, a few of which are mentioned above.

Skip forward to the current day, RSA, now employs 60 directors and produces the currently running drama The Good Wife and Numb3rs. At 74, Scott shows no sign of stopping and is working hard to support the next generations of film producers.
